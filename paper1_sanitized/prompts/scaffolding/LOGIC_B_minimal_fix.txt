## Recovery Feedback: Simplify Your Fix

Your previous fix was on the right track but had implementation errors. Think simpler.

**Action Required:**
- What is the SIMPLEST possible change that would make the test pass?
- Remove any extra logic you added that isn't strictly necessary
- Consider: can this be fixed by changing a single operator, adding one condition, or adjusting one value?
- The minimal fix is usually the correct fix — complex changes often introduce new bugs

Strip your solution down to the bare minimum that addresses the test's requirement.
