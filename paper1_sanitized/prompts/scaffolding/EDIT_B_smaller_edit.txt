## Recovery Feedback: Make a Smaller Edit

Your edit failed because it was too large or complex to match correctly. Break it down.

**Action Required:**
- Instead of replacing a large block of code, make one small change at a time
- Each edit should modify only 1-3 lines
- Apply changes sequentially — verify each one succeeds before moving to the next
- If replacing a function, do it line-by-line or in small logical chunks

Smaller, atomic edits are more likely to succeed because there's less text to match.
